## Key Interactions

### Economy → Military
- Commodities improve QOL → QOL multiplies combat effectiveness
- Resources fund mercenary training and operations
- FOREX enables cross-regional military logistics

### Military → Intelligence
- DOTC training provides operational deniability
- Military operations generate Swan Events for investigation
- Convoy escorts protect intel assets during transport

### Intelligence → Economy
- Intel reveals enemy supply chains
- Infiltration identifies high-value economic targets
- Detection of convoys enables trade interception

### Everything → Tier Progression
All systems feed into tier advancement:
- **Finance Tier** — Economic advantages compound
- **Military Tier** — Training and operations improve
- **Intelligence Tier** — Detection and analysis enhance
